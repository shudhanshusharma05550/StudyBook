NUMBER SERIES

1. Tanya is older than Eric. Cliff is older than Tanya. Eric is older than Cliff. If the first two statements are true, the third statement is


a. 7


b. 10


c. 12


d. 13


Option B is Correct. Explanation: This is a simple alternating addition and subtraction series. In the first pattern, 3 is added; in the second, 2 is subtracted.



2. Look at this series: 36, 34, 30, 28, 24, ... What number should come next?


a. 20


b. 22


c. 23


d. 26


Option B is Correct. Explanation: This is an alternating number subtraction series. First, 2 is subtracted, then 4, then 2, and so on.



3. Look at this series: 22, 21, 23, 22, 24, 23, ... What number should come next?


a. 22


b. 24


c. 25


d. 26


Option C is Correct. Explanation: In this simple alternating subtraction and addition series; 1 is subtracted, then 2 is added, and so on.



4. Look at this series: 53, 53, 40, 40, 27, 27, ... What number should come next?


a. 12


b. 14


c. 27


d. 53


Option B is Correct. Explanation: In this series, each number is repeated, then 13 is subtracted to arrive at the next number.



5. Look at this series: 21, 9, 21, 11, 21, 13, 21, ... What number should come next?


a. 14


b. 15


c. 21


d. 23


Option B is Correct. Explanation: In this alternating repetition series, the random number 21 is interpolated every other number into an otherwise simple addition series that increases by 2, beginning with the number 9.



6. Look at this series: 58, 52, 46, 40, 34, ... What number should come next?


a. 26


b. 28


c. 30


d. 32


Option B is Correct. Explanation: This is a simple subtraction series. Each number is 6 less than the previous number.



7. Look at this series: 3, 4, 7, 8, 11, 12, ... What number should come next?


a. 7


b. 10


c. 14


d. 15


Option D is Correct. Explanation: This alternating addition series begins with 3; then 1 is added to give 4; then 3 is added to give 7; then 1 is added, and so on.



8. Look at this series: 8, 22, 8, 28, 8, ... What number should come next?


a. 9


b. 29


c. 320


d. 34


Option D is Correct. Explanation: This is a simple addition series with a random number, 8, interpolated as every other number. In the series, 6 is added to each number except 8, to arrive at the next number.



9. Look at this series: 31, 29, 24, 22, 17, ... What number should come next?


a. 15


b. 14


c. 13


d. 12


Option A is Correct. Explanation: This is a simple alternating subtraction series, which subtracts 2, then 5.



10. Look at this series: 1.5, 2.3, 3.1, 3.9, ... What number should come next?


a. 4.2


b. 4.4


c. 4.7


d. 5.1


Option C is Correct. Explanation: In this simple addition series, each number increases by 0.8.
