ESSENTIAL PARTS

1. desert


a. cactus


b. arid


c. oasis


d. flat


Option B is Correct. Explantion: A desert is an arid tract of land. Not all deserts are flat (choice d). Not all deserts have cacti or oases (choices a and c).



2. harvest


a. autumn


b. stockpile


c. Stockpile


d. Tractor


Option D is Correct. Explanation: To harvest something, one must have a crop, which is the essential element for this item. Autumn (choice a) is not the only time crops are harvested



3. book


a. fiction


b. pages


c. pictures


d. learning


Option B is Correct. Explanation: The necessary part of a book is its pages; there is no book without pages. Not all books are fiction (choice a), and not all books have pictures



4. language


a. Tounge


b. Slang


c. Writing


d. Words


Option D is Correct. Explanation: Words are a necessary part of language. Slang is not necessary to language (choice b). Not all languages are written



5. school


a. student


b. report card


c. test


d. learning


Option A is Correct. Explanation: Without students, a school cannot exist; therefore, students are the essential part of schools. The other choices may be related, but they are not essential.



6. pain


a. cut


b. burn


c. nuisance


d. hurt


Option D is Correct. Explanation: Pain is suffering or hurt, so choice d is the essential element. Without hurt, there is no pain. A cut (choice a) or a burn (choice b) may cause pain, but so do many other types of injury.



7. gala


a. celebration


b. tuxedo


c. appetizer


d. orator


Option A is Correct. Explanation: A gala indicates a celebration, the necessary element here. A tuxedo (choice b) is not required garb at a gala, nor is an appetizer (choice c).



8. monopoly


a. corrupt


b. exclusive


c. rich


d. gigantic


Option B is Correct. Explanation: The essential part of a monopoly is that it involves exclusive ownership or control.



9. guitar


a. band


b. teacher


c. songs


d. strings


Option D is Correct. Explanation: A guitar does not exist without strings, so strings are an essential part of a guitar. A band is not necessary to a guitar (choice a).



10. election


a. president


b. voter


c. November


d. nation


Option B is Correct. Explanation: An election does not exist without voters. The election of a president (choice a) is a byproduct. Not all elections are held in November (choice c), nor are they nationwide (choice d).
